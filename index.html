About Us

Specialists in Hydraulic & Industrial Hose Solutions

New Star Hydraulics is a premier hose and hydraulic components supplier with branches in Pedda Amberpet, Hyderabad and Kanuru, Vijayawada. We supply a wide range of industrial hoses, hydraulic pipes, fittings, and fluid transmission accessories to industries across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Our team brings deep technical knowledge and hands-on experience to every customer interaction — helping you find exactly the right product for your application at the right price.

  • Genuine, high-quality products from trusted brands
  • Expert guidance for product selection
  • Two convenient locations for faster service
  • Competitive pricing for bulk and retail orders
